Sighting Locations

Wherever a sighting has been witnessed, we will register it and add it to our database of known locations.

This allows our users to track down and investigate for themselves what has really gone on.

As with any sightings or suspicious activity, note down the date and location as well as forwarding any photos you take. This helps us compile an accurate database that is up-to-date.
